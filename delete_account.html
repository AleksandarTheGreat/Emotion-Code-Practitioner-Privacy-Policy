Request Account Deletion

This page explains how users of the EC Practitioner app can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request deletion

You can request account and data deletion by contacting us directly:

Email: aleksandar.ecpapp@gmail.com

Please include your account identifier or the email you used inside the app so we can process your request accurately.

What data is deleted

  • Your primary account profile information
  • Any and all data you added inside the app (clients, logs, histories)

What data is retained

No personal data is retained after deletion unless strictly required for essential legal, tax, or critical security purposes.

Processing time

Deletion requests are validated and processed safely within 30 days.
