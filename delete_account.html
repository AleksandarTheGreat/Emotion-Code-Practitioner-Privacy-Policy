Request Account Deletion

This page explains how users of the Emotion Code Practitioner app can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request deletion

You can request account and data deletion by contacting us at:

emotioncodepractitioner1@gmail.com

Please include your account identifier or the email you used in the app so we can process the request.

What data is deleted

  • Your account information
  • Any data you added inside the app

What data is retained

No personal data is retained after deletion unless required for legal or security purposes.

Processing time

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
